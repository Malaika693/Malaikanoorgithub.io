Privacy Policy

JA Clinic believes in being open and upfront about how we use and look after your information. We want to assure you that your personal information is safe with us and we will never sell your details. Our privacy policy covers how we treat your data and puts you in control of what happens to it.

We are committed to protecting your personal information provided via our online platforms or given to us in other ways. You may provide personal information when you request or acquire a product or service from JA Clinic, provide a product or service to JA Clinic, complete a survey or questionnaire, enter a competition, participate in forums, or when communicating with JA Clinic by e-mail, telephone or in writing e.g. if you contact customer service or provide feedback.

This privacy policy answers all the following questions:

  • What information JA Clinic may collect about you?
  • How JA Clinic will use the information we collect about you?
  • When JA Clinic may use your details to contact you?
  • Whether JA Clinic disclose your details to anyone else?
  • How long JA Clinic will keep personal information?
  • Learn about the utilization of cookies on the JA Clinic website and discover the options available to you for rejecting these cookies.
  • How to contact JA Clinic?

JA Clinic may, from time to time, review and update this privacy policy statement to take into account new laws and technology as well as changes to JA Clinic’s operations. Please visit this page periodically to check for updates.

What information JA Clinic may collect about you?
JA Clinic collects personal information from a variety of sources, including members of the public, advertisers, prospective employees, mailing lists, recruitment agencies, contractors and business partners.

In general, the personal information JA Clinic collects includes (but is not limited to) name, address, contact details, gender, occupation, in some cases membership of professional associations, and, where relevant, certain financial information (each such item being “Personal Information”).

How JA Clinic will use information we collect about you?
JA Clinic may use Personal Information for the primary purpose for which it is collected and for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection.

JA Clinic generally uses personal information to:

  • Provide you with products or services you have requested
  • To personalise and customise your experiences on our website

JA Clinic may use Personal Information to communicate with you and to provide you with ongoing information about opportunities in which JA Clinic believes you may be interested.

In most cases where JA Clinic asks you to provide Personal Information, if you do not provide JA Clinic with that information, JA Clinic will not be able to provide you with the requested product or service.

When JA Clinic may use your details to contact you?
JA Clinic may contact you:

  • In relation to the functioning of any service you have signed up for in order to ensure that JA Clinic can deliver the services to you
  • Where you have opted to receive further correspondence
